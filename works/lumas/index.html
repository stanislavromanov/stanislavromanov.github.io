Works > Lumas (ArtStream)

I was hired for back-end infrastructure of Artstream, a platform that streams curated artwork to screens. My role involved building serverless functions with AWS Lambda to process images into frames, leveraging tools like Headless-Chrome and FFmpeg for advanced rendering. These processed images were stored in Amazon S3, with DynamoDB handling metadata storage for efficient access. The infrastructure supported seamless delivery of high-quality visual content to users, enabling a smooth and engaging experience for art lovers across devices.

Year: 2023
Tech: NodeAWS LambdaDynamoDB
